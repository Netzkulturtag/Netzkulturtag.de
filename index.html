Netzkulturtag

The official holiday for all digitalism people of the world.

The Technology- and Nerdblogs http://mblTme.de/ and http://leetperium.de/ together with the IT-Security Forum http://forum.mbiteme.de/ proudly present the NETZKULTURTAG (Netcultureday)!
The Netzkulturtag (Netcultureday) – in honour of all nerds and geeks who cling together the internet and care for functional “…technology”! This is an idea to unite the makers, creators and activists of the „digital world“.
The aim is to honour the, often only inadequate noticed, „heroes“ of the internet, who care for servers, software and content and provide those mostly free for charge. Without the nerds, the internet and the entire digital world in it’s present form would be unthinkable.

This holiday isn’t only intended for computer-freaks, but also for all freckler, technicians, electricians and e.g. designers of all kinds!

We, the tech-savvy people of this earth, determine the future, for who nowadays lives without the internet, social media or an automatic coffee maker?

Those people who do something for the technological progress every day, shall be honoured and honour themselves that day!

The Netzkulturtag (Netcultureday) shall be dated as the 6th August. On the 6th August 1991 the hypertext-project named „World Wide Web“, by Tim Berners-Lee, was published in a newsgroup and available worldwide. Hence this day can be seen as the “founding day” of the internet as we know it nowadays.
